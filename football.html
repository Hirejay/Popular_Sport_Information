Football

  Football, the most popular sport on the planet, is a mystery in India. Football is predominantly a spectator sport in India, dubbed the "sleeping giant" of the footballing world by former FIFA president Sepp Blatter. While the ISL has a sizable fan base in India, it is the massive following of European club championships that is the source of football's appeal in the country.Currently, India's football team is ranked 105th in the world.
